# Photography shot list

The site currently runs on curated stock comps (`lib/images.ts` — every slot
verified and swappable in one line). Real project photography is what turns
"looks premium" into "books surveys". Work through this list across your next
three jobs; a modern phone camera shot in good light is entirely sufficient.

## The golden rules

- **Golden hour or overcast.** Midday sun kills paving texture. The hour before
  sunset makes porcelain and brass planting glow — half the shots below want
  that light.
- **Wet the paving first.** Lightly hosed porcelain/sandstone photographs
  dramatically better — colour deepens, texture reads.
- **Tidy ruthlessly.** No hose reels, wheelie bins, trampolines or cement bags.
- **Shoot level, at chest height,** phone held straight (no tilt-down snapshots).
  Use the grid; keep verticals vertical.
- **Same-position before/after pairs.** Before starting ANY job, take 3 photos
  from marked positions (mark them — a photo of your feet works). Repeat exactly
  at handover. These pairs power the sliders and are the highest-converting
  asset on the site.

## Per-slot list (maps 1:1 to `lib/images.ts`)

| Slot | The shot |
|---|---|
| `heroHome` | Signature completed garden, golden hour, wide, from the corner — patio + lawn + planting + house together |
| `patioDining` | Porcelain terrace with dining set, shot low across the slabs toward the house |
| `patioCourtyard` | Courtyard/garden-room build, showing edge detail and jointing |
| `pavingCircle` | Detail: sett circle, border course or step bullnose — craft close-up |
| `pavingBlockTexture` | Top-down block/porcelain texture, wet, 1m² frame-fill |
| `driveEstate` | Finished driveway from the road, house centred, cars OFF the drive |
| `deckBoards` | Composite boards raking light close-up, hidden-fixing edge visible |
| `deckWhite` | Full deck scene with furniture, steps and lighting |
| `pergolaLouvre` | Pergola with seating beneath, shot from inside the garden |
| `pergolaDark` | Pergola + fencing lines together, dusk if possible |
| `lawnStudio` | Fresh lawn with crisp edge against paving/beds |
| `turfPanel` / `turfBeds` | Artificial lawn meeting porcelain/sleeper edging — the junction is the shot |
| `raisedBeds` | Sleeper beds planted up, corner detail visible |
| `planting` | Bed at peak season, layered heights, shallow depth of field |
| `redesignAfter` / `redesignModern` | The transformation money-shots — same angle as the "before" |
| `wildBefore` | The honest "before": overgrown, broken concrete, the worse the better |
| `craft*` (×3) | The team mid-graft: screeding, laying, cutting, planting. Hands, levels, string lines — authenticity gold |
| `lightBollard` / `lightModern` | Blue hour, lights on, tripod or steady rest — shoot 20 minutes after sunset |
