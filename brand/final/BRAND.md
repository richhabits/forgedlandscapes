# Forged Landscapes — Brand Identity

_Lane A “Heritage Estate.” Engraved English estate marque — old money, quietly confident. EST. 2026 · motto **FORGED PROPERLY**._

## The system
| Asset | File | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Primary crest | `crest.svg` | Hero, OG, certificates, van door, formal footer. Never below ~120px tall. |
| FL monogram | `monogram.svg` | Standalone mark — favicons-large, stamps, embossing, watermark. |
| Wordmark | `wordmark.svg` | Headers, letterhead, email signature. |
| Roundel seal | `roundel.svg` | Social avatars, stickers, wax-seal treatment. Reads to 48px. |
| Favicon | `favicon.svg` | Browser tab / app icon — radically simplified anvil. |
| Pattern | `pattern.svg` | Section headers, portal chrome, livery. Subtle, low opacity. |

## Colour (gold is an accent, never a fill)
- **Racing green** `#0B3D2E` — primary ground · **Forge-950** `#10140f` — deepest ground (matches site)
- **Ivory** `#F5F0E6` / **Bone** `#ede6d6` — primary ink & paper
- **Aged gold** `#B08D4A` — hairlines, keylines, the single forge spark. Nothing more.
- **Charcoal** `#232323` — mono ink on white
Tokens in `tokens.css`; they map to the live site's forge/brass/bone scale — one system.

## Four colourways (every mark)
1. **Full colour** — ivory ink on racing green
2. **All-gold on green** — gold ink on racing green
3. **All-charcoal on white** — for documents, invoices, faxable marks
4. **All-ivory on dark** — reversed, for photography overlays
Pre-built in `colourways/`.

## Type
- Display / wordmark: high-contrast Garamond in letterspaced caps (Cormorant Garamond or EB Garamond), tracking ≥ 0.12em.
- In logo files, type is converted to outline paths so lockups never depend on an installed font.

## Clearspace & minimums
- Keep clearspace of one “cap-height” of the wordmark around every mark.
- Minimums: crest 120px tall · wordmark 140px wide · roundel 48px · favicon 16px.

## Don’t
- Don’t fill areas with gold. Don’t recolour outside the four colourways.
- Don’t stretch, rotate, add shadows/gradients/bevels, or place the mark on a busy photo without the dark scrim.
- Don’t rebuild the FL monogram — it’s drawn to be ownable and registrable.
